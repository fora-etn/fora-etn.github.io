## Safety assurance of Fog-based industrial systems

**Host:** [Mälardalen University College (MDH)](/partners/mdh/), [School of Innovation, Design and Engineering](http://www.mdh.se/idt?l=en_UK).

- Main supervisor: Prof. Sasikumar Punnekkat, [sasikumar.punnekkat@mdh.se](mailto:sasikumar.punnekkat@mdh.se) (contact person)
- Co-supervisor: Prof. Hans Hansson hans.hansson@mdh.se

**Objectives:**

1. Enable efficient safety certification of Fog-based industrial automation systems by extending the state-of-the-art safety case approaches.
2. Develop a safety assurance framework for Fog-based industrial systems that reduces costs and takes into account security.

**Visits and collaboration:**

- TTT (Dr. Wilfried Steiner): Develop a proof-of-concept safety assurance of a Fog Infrastructure use case.
- ABB (Dr. Magnus Larsson): Learn about certification standards in industrial automation and safety assurance practice.
