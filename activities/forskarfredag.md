## Forskarfredag 2021

2021, September 23rd, Virtual

![](/assets/uploads/2022/01/Forskarfredag.png)

Forskarfredag is a Swedish equivalent of European Researchers’ Night. ESRs from MDH prepared a presentation covering thier research topics and presented their research to the audience. In the talk they discussed about the spread of IoT devices, cloud computing and novel edge and fog computing approaches. They also motivated their talk with some interesting cutting edge applications that can benefit from the paradigm.

The following ESRs participated in Forskarfredag:

- Nitin Desai
- Vaclav Struhar
- Lan Dao
- Zeinab Bakhshi
- Salman Shaik

More info is avialable via [www.forskarfredag.se](https://forskarfredag.se/)
