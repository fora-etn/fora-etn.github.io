## FORA Project Meeting (June 2020)

2020, June 15th, Virtual

This was an online internal review meeting of the FORA beneficiaries in conjunction with the Training School 3 (TS3). Before this meeting we collected materials on the ESR progress (their status) and on the progress of the action (as presentations and videos). We discussed the following in the meeting:

- The progress of our ESRs. We looked at their updated CDPs, the number of ECTs obtained and required, and the number of publications (accepted, under review, submitted).
- We reviewed the information package to be sent for the Advisory Board (AB) which were asked to provide feedback on our progress.
- We reviewed the progress on communication, dissemination, and exploitation activities.
- We reviewed the structure and planning of the upcoming deliverables.
