## FORA Training Day

2018, December 12th, Copenhagen

![](/assets/uploads/2018/12/Picture1.jpg)

The FORA’s interim check meeting has taken place Dec. 12, at Park Inn By Radisson hotel, Copenhagen. In conjunction with this meeting, we had a training day on Dec.11 to define the main concepts of the Fog Computing platform and plan its development and use. We had 32 participants, with representatives from each beneficiary.

The local organizers of this event were Prof. Paul Pop and Dr. Bahram Zarrin from the Technical University of Denmark.

**The following items have been on the agenda:**

- Welcome message and overview of FORA Training Day and Interim Review (Paul Pop, DTU)
- [OpenFog Reference Architecture, Use Cases and CISCO’s Edge Computing](https://drive.google.com/open?id=142M-90jVshp7KBwNxKRyxH9OdCQ7CAeu) (Frank Michaud/CISCO Switzerland)
- [The MFN100 Fog Node from TTTech and evaluation of hypervisors](https://drive.google.com/open?id=1of6HxDXQDpd9n1WIqD2N8vZ7i_cWDY5W) (Jan Ruh/TTTech Computertechnik AG)
- [TSN: interaction with the End System and scheduling time-sensitive frames](https://drive.google.com/open?id=1BoffFJs5Ax7jCwuPCuFACdO7Hub9uzsp) (Wilfried Steiner/TTTech Computertechnik AG)
- [OpenStack for the Edge (Syed Moneeb Javed/Ericsson)](https://drive.google.com/open?id=1S3cHsFWuS47P8Ir7byHLSddsvjssQHfA)
- iFogSim simulator (Bahram Zarin/DTU, Denmark)
- FORA Fog Computing Platform Requirements and research collaboration (Paul Pop/DTU, Denmark)
- [Literature survey for the state-of-the-art March deliverable](https://drive.google.com/open?id=1GHRwaQ4aJboUMrHHj6NJ57nYnPRujfxC) (Stefan Schulte/TU Wien, Austria)
