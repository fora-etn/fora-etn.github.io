[TTTech Computertechnik AG (TTT)](https://www.tttech.com/) is the leading supplier of dependable networking solutions based on time-triggered technology. The company is headquartered in Vienna, Austria, and employs ca. 500 employees. TTT is engaged in the further development of dependable networking technology in the domains space, aerospace, automotive and industrial control systems. (TTT) provides, as the industrial beneficiary partner, longstanding expertise in the field of dependable real time systems and safety-critical system development.

TTT has actively engaged in the further development of interdisciplinary IT/OT technology, for example, an automotive Ethernet switch that integrates TTT specifics (OT) with standard Ethernet technology (IT). TTT plans to embed the ESRs in practical research and development tasks, such that they are permanently exposed to real -world project situations and will thus benefit from: (a) the existing research knowledge shared in the company (b) working directly with senior company researchers.

As an active member of the IEEE 802.1 [Time-Sensitive Networking (TSN) standardization’s group](https://www.tttech.com/technologies/deterministic-ethernet/time-sensitive-networking/?gclid=CjwKEAjw07nJBRDG_tvshefHhWQSJABRcE-Zz617L0MP_n02HuXx4A68drRQRkIYLu7CrtcY4VGj4hoCGzfw_wcB), TTT can provide the ESR with first-hand information regarding the latest standardization developments. TTT can likewise contribute by:

- Keeping track of any advances in the field that could modify any aspects of the training program.
- Introducing the latest findings obtained within FORA to the TSN standardization committee.

Founded as a spin-off of Vienna University of Technology, TTT also has deep insights into the scientific education process and can thus structurally and personally support the education of academics, e.g., by establishing buddy concepts between the ESRs and scientific staff at the company.
