[Volvo Construction Equipment AB](https://www.volvoce.com/) is part of the Volvo Group and among the world’s leading manufacturers of articulated haulers and wheel loaders, and one the world’s foremost manufacturers of excavation equipment, road development machines and compact construction equipment. One important research area for Volvo Construction Equipment is autonomous or semi-autonomous machines.
