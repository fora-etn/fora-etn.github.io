15 PhD positions are available at 7 partners in Sweden, Denmark, Germany and Austria in the European Training Network [FORA—Fog Computing for Robotics and Industrial Automation](/).

Please see below the description of each PhD project, and apply for the positions. Please [read the FORA research objectives](/research-objectives/) before you apply for a position. When applying, please apply to a specific partner and for a specific project, based on your interests and background.
