The research in FORA has resulted in several technology bricks that can be used in building Fog Computing solutions for Industrial Automation. These technology bricks consist of reference architectures, models, software and hardware prototypes, mechanisms and protocols, etc.

Please contact [Dr. Mohammadreza Barzegaran](http://www.dtu.dk/english/service/phonebook/Person?id=132894&cpid=238661&tab=2) for the licensing details. We list below the main technology bricks produced by the FORA ESRs. Further details are available in the ESR web pages, and in the related deliverables which can be downloaded from [here](/deliverables/).
