**FORA — Fog Computing for Robotics and Industrial Automation** was a European Training Network (ETN) funded under the EU Horizon 2020 Marie Skłodowska-Curie programme. It funded and trained 15 PhD candidates in the area of Fog Computing during 2017–2021.

## About

FORA—Fog Computing for Robotics and Industrial Automation was a European Training Network (ETN), which funded and trained 15 PhD candidates in the area of Fog Computing, during the period 2017–2021.

[Read more about FORA](/about-fora/)

## Vacancies

The FORA ETN was specifically created to boost your research career; read here about [the benefits](/etn-benefits/). The PhD positions were filled during 2017–2018; the [vacancies page](/vacancies/) is kept as a historical record.

## Partners

The partners (beneficiaries) in the project were: Technical University of Denmark (DTU), Vienna University of Technology (TU Wien), Mälardalen University (MDH), Technical University of Kaiserslautern (TUKL), TTTech, SYSGO and ABB.
